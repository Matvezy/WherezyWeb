What is Wherezy

We are Wherezy, a fun customizable location tracking bracelet for young children that connects with an app on the parent’s phone,
providing parents an extra sense of security regarding their child’s safety.

Core Values

  • Secure

    Not only is Wherezy incentivizing for children to wear due to the fun designs, it also has a lock and key mechanism to prevent the child from taking it off.

  • Precise

    Wherezy uses GPS, Wifi, Cellular, and Bluetooth to create a precise and accurate location of your child’s location.

  • Stylish

    Wherezy is customizable making it appealing and fun for the user.
